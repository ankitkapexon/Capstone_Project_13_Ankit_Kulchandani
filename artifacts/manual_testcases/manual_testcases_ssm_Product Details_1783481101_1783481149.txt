Screen: Product Details

Test Case 1: Verify product details are displayed correctly
Description: Validate that the Product Details screen shows the key product information needed for purchase decisions.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen for any product.
  2. Observe the Product image section.
  3. Observe the Product title text.
  4. Observe the Price text.
  5. Observe the Rating text.
Expected Result:
  - The Product image is visible.
  - The Product title is visible and readable.
  - The Price is visible and formatted as a price value.
  - The Rating is visible if available for the product.
  - The screen presents product information clearly without missing or overlapping elements.

Test Case 2: Verify Quantity selector increments product quantity
Description: Ensure the Quantity selector allows the user to increase the quantity before purchase.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Note the default value shown in the Quantity selector.
  3. Tap the increment control on the Quantity selector once.
  4. Tap the increment control again.
Expected Result:
  - The quantity increases by one on each tap.
  - The updated quantity is displayed correctly.
  - No UI distortion or delay occurs while updating the quantity.

Test Case 3: Verify Quantity selector decrements product quantity
Description: Ensure the Quantity selector allows the user to reduce the selected quantity properly.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Increase the Quantity selector value to more than 1.
  3. Tap the decrement control on the Quantity selector once.
  4. Tap the decrement control again if the value is still above the minimum.
Expected Result:
  - The quantity decreases by one on each tap.
  - The displayed quantity always matches the performed action.
  - The control remains responsive.

Test Case 4: Verify Quantity selector does not go below minimum allowed value
Description: Validate that the user cannot reduce the quantity below the allowed minimum, typically 1.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Set the Quantity selector to its lowest visible value.
  3. Tap the decrement control again.
Expected Result:
  - The quantity does not go below the minimum allowed value.
  - No negative or zero quantity is shown if the minimum is 1.
  - The app handles the action gracefully without errors.

Test Case 5: Verify Add to Cart adds the selected quantity
Description: Confirm that tapping Add to Cart adds the product using the quantity chosen on the Product Details screen.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Set the Quantity selector to a value greater than 1.
  3. Tap Add to Cart.
Expected Result:
  - The selected product is added to the cart.
  - The quantity added matches the quantity selected on the screen.
  - A success indication, cart update, or navigation behavior occurs as per app design.

Test Case 6: Verify Add to Cart works with default quantity
Description: Validate that Add to Cart works correctly when the user does not change the default quantity.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Do not modify the Quantity selector.
  3. Tap Add to Cart.
Expected Result:
  - The product is added to the cart with the default quantity.
  - The action completes successfully without requiring quantity adjustment.

Test Case 7: Verify Buy Now initiates immediate purchase flow
Description: Ensure the Buy Now button takes the user toward the immediate checkout or purchase flow for the displayed product.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Set the Quantity selector to a desired quantity.
  3. Tap Buy Now.
Expected Result:
  - The app proceeds to the next purchase-related step, such as checkout, order summary, or payment flow.
  - The selected product and quantity are carried forward correctly.

Test Case 8: Verify Favorite icon toggles product favorite state
Description: Confirm that the Favorite action can mark and unmark a product as a favorite.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Details screen.
  2. Tap the Favorite icon once.
  3. Observe any change in icon state or feedback.
  4. Tap the Favorite icon again.
Expected Result:
  - The first tap marks the product as favorite.
  - The icon or screen feedback reflects the saved favorite state.
  - The second tap removes the product from favorites or returns it to the original state.

Test Case 9: Verify Back button returns to previous screen
Description: Ensure the Back icon provides correct backward navigation from the Product Details screen.
Steps:
  1. Navigate to the Product Details screen from a previous screen.
  2. Tap the Back icon.
Expected Result:
  - The app returns to the previous screen.
  - No unexpected app exit, freeze, or wrong navigation occurs.
