Screen: Product Listing

Test Case 1: Verify Product Listing screen loads with key browsing controls
Description: Validate that the Product Listing screen displays the core UI components required for catalog browsing and product discovery.
Steps:
  1. Launch the app and navigate to the Product Listing screen.
  2. Observe the top and main content areas of the screen.
  3. Verify the presence of the Search field.
  4. Verify the presence of the Category filter control.
  5. Verify the presence of the Sort button.
  6. Verify that at least one Product item card is displayed in the listing area.
Expected Result:
  - The Product Listing screen opens successfully.
  - The Search field is visible and accessible.
  - The Category filter control is visible and tappable.
  - The Sort button is visible and tappable.
  - Product item cards are displayed for browsing.

Test Case 2: Search for a product using the Search field
Description: Verify that users can search the catalog by entering a product keyword in the Search field.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Tap the Search field.
  3. Enter a valid product keyword.
  4. Submit or wait for the search results to refresh.
  5. Review the displayed Product item cards.
Expected Result:
  - The Search field accepts text input.
  - The product list refreshes based on the entered keyword.
  - Displayed Product item cards are relevant to the search term.

Test Case 3: Verify search with partial keyword input
Description: Validate that the Search field supports partial text input and returns matching or related product results.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Tap the Search field.
  3. Enter a partial product name or incomplete keyword.
  4. Submit the search or wait for the listing to update.
  5. Review the visible Product item cards.
Expected Result:
  - The Search field accepts partial text.
  - The listing updates without error.
  - Matching or related Product item cards are shown when applicable.

Test Case 4: Verify search with non-matching keyword
Description: Validate behavior when the user searches for a product that does not exist in the catalog.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Tap the Search field.
  3. Enter a random or invalid keyword that is unlikely to match any product.
  4. Submit the search or wait for the results to refresh.
  5. Observe the listing area.
Expected Result:
  - The app handles the non-matching search gracefully.
  - No crash or UI corruption occurs.
  - The screen shows either no Product item cards or an appropriate empty state/message.

Test Case 5: Verify search field can be cleared and product list resets
Description: Ensure that clearing the Search field restores the default product catalog view.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Use the Search field to enter a valid keyword and confirm filtered results appear.
  3. Clear the text from the Search field.
  4. Dismiss the keyboard if needed.
  5. Observe the Product item list.
Expected Result:
  - The Search field text is removed successfully.
  - The product list resets to the default or broader catalog state.
  - Product item cards are shown according to the unfiltered listing.

Test Case 6: Apply Category filter to narrow product results
Description: Verify that the Category filter can be used to refine the product listing.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Tap the Category filter control.
  3. Select an available category option.
  4. Confirm or apply the filter if required.
  5. Review the visible Product item cards.
Expected Result:
  - The Category filter opens successfully.
  - A category can be selected.
  - The product listing refreshes to show only products relevant to the selected category.

Test Case 7: Change category filter selection
Description: Validate that users can switch from one category to another and see updated product results.
Steps:
  1. Open the Product Listing screen.
  2. Tap Category filter and select one category.
  3. Verify the product list updates.
  4. Tap Category filter again.
  5. Select a different category.
  6. Review the updated Product item cards.
Expected Result:
  - The second category selection is accepted.
  - The listing refreshes according to the newly selected category.
  - Results change appropriately between category selections.
